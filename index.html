Your Mantras

Use these mantras as a guide to help you find inspiration, understanding, and calm during these difficult times

Water Mantra

Water as an element is associated with emotion, imagination, and intuition. Water is both gentle and powerful. It takes the form of what surrounds it.

Earth Mantra

The earth element is believed to represent pragmatism, heaviness, matter, and the terrestrial world. The Earth tribe is not known for its religious beleifs but rather its worldiness and focus on the practical concerns of life.

Fire Mantra

Fire is the sign of passion, assertiveness, & action. Tap into it for power to overcome obstacles and break down barriers!

Air Mantra

Air is beleived to represent breath, life, communication, spirit, intelligence, creativity, and new beginnings. Focus on air to release your creative energy!
